7-Day Data Science Bootcamp

This intensive 7-day Data Science Bootcamp is designed to take beginners on a journey from raw data to meaningful insights, empowering them with the skills to analyze, visualize, and make data-driven decisions. Whether you’re a student, entrepreneur, freelancer, or career switcher, this hands-on program provides a complete foundation in the rapidly growing field of Data Science.

Participants will learn core concepts such as statistics, data wrangling, data visualization, exploratory analysis, and predictive modeling. You’ll also gain practical experience with popular tools and frameworks like Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Scikit-learn. By the end of the program, you’ll be equipped to clean, analyze, and model real-world datasets—culminating in a major capstone project: “Data-Driven Health Insights and Predictions.”
